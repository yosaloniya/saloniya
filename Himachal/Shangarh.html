Shangarh Jibhi Tour Package

6 days / 5 Nights

Day 01 : Jaipur to Aut

Once you reach Jaipur, you will have to proceed towards your decided boarding point. Upon reaching, you will then be boarding an AC Volvo bus / Private vehicle as per the group size and availability for an overnight journey to Aut

Day 02 : Aut to Shangarh

After an overnight journey, reach the Aut Tunnel. From here, take a one-hour drive through the lush green forested area towards the Sainj Valley. Located in the Sainj Valley of Himachal Pradesh, Shangarh is famous for the rich history that is visible in the architecture all around the sm,all town.. On reaching Shangarh, check in to the hotel and take some rest. Then you can visit the various temples like the Sangchul Mahadev Temple, that is a bold brown structure located in the middle of the sea of green grass. You can also visit the Tower Temple that showcases extraordinary wood craftsmanship. In the evening you can visit the settlements of Goshati and Darari that are lined with eye-pleasing apple orchards. You can then return back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 03 : Excursion to Pundrik Rishi Lake
Wake up to a beautiful view of green landscapes and have a delicious breakfast. You'll then get ready and embark on a hike to the Pundrik Rishi Lake. Start your journey towards the village of Jhili Neahi, from where you will be following a trail that will lead you through the beautiful landscapes. Following the trail, you'll ultimately reach the beautiful village known as Sarahan with open meadows. Pass through the cedar trees and maize farms to reach a small shrine and ultimately to the Pundrik Rishi Lake. After spending some quiet time here, you'll be retracing the footsteps back towards Shangarh. Overnight stay at the hotel in Shangarh.

Day 04 : Shangarh to Jibhi Valley & Sightseeing
After a delicious breakfast, you'll start your journey towards Jibhi. You'll be driving through Downhill in the Sainj Valley and then follow the Tirthan river to reach the beautiful village of Jibhi. Located in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh, Jibhi is an alluring village surrounded by natural beauty and gives you the taste of an offbeat destination. On reaching Jibhi, check in to the hotel and take some rest. Then you can go out exploring this beautiful hamlet on your own. Visit Jibhi Waterfall and trek to the nearby small town known as Mini Thailand. You can try out trout fishing, and visit the Great Himalayan National park. Overnight stay at your hotel in Jibhi.

Day 05 : Excursion to Serolsar Lake
Wake up to a beautiful sunrise and have a delicious cup of tea. After having a wholesome breakfast, check out from the hotel and embark on the beautiful journey through the stunning Jibhi - Jalori Pass. Here, you can do a short hike to Serolsar Lake. Serolsar Lake is a beautiful marvel that quietly sits in the laps of nature. Surrounded by Alpine trees, the Serolsar Lake is held sacred by the locals and is also famous for the nearby temple dedicated to Goddess Budhi Nagin. You will then trek back to the Jalori pass from where you will be returning back to Jibhi. Once done exploring, you will embark towards Aut Tunnel from where you will be boarding your overnight AC Volvo bus / Private vehicle as per the group size and availability back to Jaipur.

Day 06 : Arrival at Jaipur
After passing through the hairpin bends and beautiful scenery, you will reach back to Jaipuir in the morning. Once you arrive in Jaipur, your amazing trip to Mahi Tour And Travels Valley comes to an end.
