ManaliKasol Tour Package from Delhi

5 days / 4 Nights

Day 01 : Delhi to Manali

Arrive at a pre-arranged location in Delhi, from where you'll board an AC sleeper bus bound for Manali. The overnight cruise will take you through some of the best sights as you head into the majestic Himalayas to reach Manali the next morning.

Day 02 : Arrival and Sightseeing in Manali

When you arrive in Manali, a representative will be waiting to assist you with being transferred to a hotel that you have reserved. Complete the check-in procedure, then take a moment to relax. The enigmatic Hadimba Devi Temple, usually known as the heart of Manali and set among the lovely Deodar trees, will be your first stop. Afterwards, you'll go to the Manu Temple and the Vashisht Temple to feel the spiritual energy. The Tibetan Monastery is a well-known site that you will see. It is surrounded by stores selling carpets and handicrafts. You are then free to wander around the Mall street in the evening, where you may go to some of Manali's greatest cafes or go on a shopping spree. Back to the hotel for dinner in the evening. Stay the night at Manali.

Day 03 : Manali to Solang Valley
A nice breakfast awaits you when you awake. You will set off on your Solang Valley trip today. Solang Valley, commonly referred to as "Snow Valley," is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. The valley is famous for its beauty and accommodates a variety of winter adventure sports. You can try your hand at adventure sports like snowboarding, paragliding, and zorbing in this captivating snow valley. Return to the hotel for a substantial dinner after an action-packed day. Stay the night at Manali.

Day 04 : Excursion to Kasol & Departure
The day begins early as you awaken and immediately begin heading to the lovely town of Kullu after breakfast. Spend some time in the lovely Kullu valley, stop by the rabbit farm and the Kullu Shawl Factory, and then go to Kasol. Arrive in Kasol, a mysterious village that is referred to as "Mini Israel of India" and where you may enjoy visiting the lovely market. Additionally, you can go cafe-hopping among the many eateries that offer delectable cuisine. You might then travel to Manikaran, a famous Sikh destination noted for its hot springs and gurudwara, Manikaran Sahib. Take a refreshing dip in the hot spring, which is thought to have many therapeutic advantages and will revitalise your body and soul. Return to Manali in the evening so you can catch a bus headed back to Delhi.

Day 05 : Reach Delhi
You'll return to Delhi in the morning after navigating the winding roads and breathtaking surroundings. Your incredible trip to Manali and Kasol ends when you get to Delhi.
