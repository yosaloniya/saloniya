Shimla Manali Honeymoon Tour

7 days / 6 Nights

Day 01 : Jaipur to Manali

Reach a predetermined spot in Jaipur where a representative will greet you and help you with the smooth transfer from Jaipur to Manali. The overnight journey takes you through some of the most scenic views as you travel to the majestic Himalayas to reach Manali the next morning.

Day 02 : Sightseeing at Manali

Reach Manali where you will be meeting a representative who will help you get transferred to your pre-booked hotel. Complete the check-in formalities and rest for some time. You will first visit the mystical Hadimba Devi Temple, which locals refer to as the heart of Manali and is located amidst the beautiful Deodar trees. You will then proceed to visit the Manu Temple, Vashisht Temple to feel the spiritual aura. Surrounded by shops selling handicrafts and carpets, the Tibetan Monastery is a famous sight that you will visit. Later, in the evening you are free to explore the Mall road, where you can visit some of the best cafes in Manali or indulge in a shopping spree. In the evening, return back to the hotel for dinner. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 03 : Excursion to Solang Valley
Wake up and treat your taste buds with a yummy breakfast. Today you will be heading out for your Solang Valley tour which is sometimes also called “Snow Valley” and is a haven for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers. Start your day by visiting sightseeing points of Kothi, Gorge, Gulaba and Marhi Proceed for an excursion to the picturesque Solang Valley The valley hosts various winter adventure sports and is admired for its beauty. This mesmerizing snow valley offers some adventure activities such as snowboarding, paragliding and zorbing to try your hands on. After a day full of thrill and excitement, return back to the hotel for a hearty dinner. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 04 : Excursion to Manikaran & Kasol
The day starts early as you will wake up and just after breakfast will start travelling towards the beautiful town of Kullu Spend some time in the beautiful Kullu valley and creating a snowman and rafting through the icy waters. Visit the Kullu Shawl Factory and the Rabbit farm and later head towards Manikaran. While travelling to Manikaran pass through the mystic town of Kasol, popularly known as the “Mini Israel of India”. Manikaran is an important site for Sikhs and is famous for the Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara and the hot water springs. Refresh yourself as you take a dip in the hot spring that is believed to have many medicinal benefits and will rejuvenate your body and soul. In the evening, return back to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay in Manali.

Day 05 : Manali to Shimla
In the morning after breakfast, complete the check out from the hotel. Start your journey towards Shimla by Deluxe Bus Enjoy the scenic views en-route and upon arrival in Shimla check in to the hotel. Later head out to explore the heart of the Queen of Hills. Spend your evening by strolling around Mall Road, Ridge, Church, Lakkar Bazar. Return back to the hotel for dinner & Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 06 : Shimla and Kufri Sightseeing
Wake up in the morning and have a hearty breakfast. Complete the check-out formalities at the hotel before heading out towards Kufri. You will pass through the splendid Hasan Valley that is known for its scenic beauty and pleasant weather. Enjoy a visit to Green Valley and Fagu Valley to get some spectacular Himalayan views. Visit the Himalayan Mini Zoo which is home to wildlife like Felines, Antelopes, Monal, and other birds, and the nearby Chini Bungalow. Indulge in some fun activities at Indira Gandhi tourist park and Fun World. Later in the return back to Shimla to board an overnight Volvo to Delhi.

Day 07 : Reach Jaipur
After passing through the hairpin bends and beautiful scenery, you will reach back to Jaipur in the morning. Once you arrive in Jaipur, your amazing trip comes to an end.
