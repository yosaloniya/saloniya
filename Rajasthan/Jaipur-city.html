JAIPUR CITY TOUR

3 days

Day 01 : Jaipur (Arrival)

Pickup from Jaipur Airport, transfer to Hotel. Later after fresh-up enjoy Jaipur City Tour visit Jalmahal, Step well of Amer, Meera Temple & Amer fort. Later enjoy Light & Sound show of Amer fort & back to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 02 : Jaipur

Today visit Birla Temple, Albert Hall, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar evening visit Patrika Gate & enjoy dinner with folk dance & Music in Chokhi Dhani resort. Later back to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 03 : Jaipur (Drop)
Today morning after breakfast & Checkout visit Jaigarh Fort, Nahargarh fort & enjoy Leopard Safari in Jaipur. Evening drop at Jaipur Airport.
