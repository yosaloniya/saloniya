Unseen Rajasthan Tour

05 Nights - 06 days

Day 01 : Jaipur Arrival

Welcome on arrival, transfer to hotel later after fresh-up visit Hawa Mahal, City Palace,Jantar-Mantar, Amber Fort & Jalmahal evening visit Birla Temple and explore Jaipurmarket later back to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 02 : jaipur - Ranthambore ( 4 Hrs )

Today morning after breakfast & checkout drive to Ranthambore, reach & transfer to hotel, later enjoy afternoon Ranthambore wild safari by Jeep/Canter. Take beautiful pictures of wild animals in their natural activities. Evening back to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 03 : Ranthambore - Kota ( 3 hrs )

Today morning drive to Kota, reach & transfer to Hotel, later after fresh-up visit Seven wonder’s park, Kota Bridge, Garadia Mahadev Temple (Island of Rajasthan), Evening back to hotel for overnight stay.

Day 04 : Kota - Chittorgarh - ( Rani Padmini Palace ) ( 4 Hrs )

Today morning after breakfast & checkout drive to Chittorgarh, transfer to hotel. After fresh-up visit Chittorgarh Fort, Vijay Stambh, Meera Temple & Rani Padmini Palace. Evening back to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 05 : Chittorgarh - Udaipur ( 2 Hrs )

Today morning visit Amber fort, enjoy Elephant Ride ( Optional ride on direct payment basis @ Rs.1000 per Elephant for 2 pax) Visit Durga Temple, Mirror Palace, Royal ladies apartments and many more in Amber fort, later visit Jalmahal & after lunch free for shopping. Evening drop at Jaipur Airport / Railway Station.

Day 06 : Udaipur ( Drop )

Today we will drop you at Udaipur Airport / Railway Station. Or Drive back to Jaipur, reach & Transfer to Jaipur Railway Station / Airport in Evening.
