PACKAGES

Rajasthan Tour Green Zone

04 Nights - 05 days

Day 01 : Udaipur

Welcome on arrival, transfer to hotel after fresh up visit City Palace - Standing on the bank of lake Pichola, are massive series of palaces built at different time commencing from 1559 A.D. The balconies of the palace provide panoramic view of "Jag Niwas" (Now world wide famous Lake palace hotel), Jag Mandir on one side and on the other the city of Udaipur, Lake Pichola enjoy boat ride, later visit Jagdish Temple, Jag Mandir & Pratap Smark (Moti Magri), Vintage Car Museum, Queen’s Garden, Evening back to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 02 : Udaipur - Mount Abu ( 4 Hrs )

Today morning drive to Mount Abu, reach & transfer to hotel, later enjoy visiting Nakki Lake, Om Shanti Bhawan, Delwara Jain Temple, Achalgarh Fort, Guru Shikhar & Gau Mukh Temple & Sun-Set Point. Evening back to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 03 : Mount Abu - Jodhpur ( 6 hrs )

Today morning after breakfast drive to Jodhpur, reach & transfer to hotel, after fresh-up visit Mehrangarh Fort this is the most glorious fort in the city of Jodhpur. Built in the year 1459 by Rao Jodha, the fort includes several palaces, temples, galleries and a museum within its premises. & Jashwant Thada - this is a royal white marbled cenotaph. Mainly built in the memory of Maharaja Jaswant Singh, this place even displays some of the rare portraits of Jodhpur's former rulers. Later visit Umed Bhawan Palace - Constructed in the 20th Century by Maharaja Umaid Singh, this is one of the captivating Jodhpur palaces. However, presently, a part of the Umaid Bhavan Palace has been transformed to a museum and a hotel. Evening free to explore Jodhpur markets & transfer to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 04 : Jodhpur - Ajmer - Pushkar - Jaipur ( 6 Hrs )

Today morning drive to Jaipur, visit Khwaja Sahib Dargah in Ajmer, Pushkar Lake & Bhrama Temple & proceed to Jaipur, reach & transfer to hotel. After fresh-up visit City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal & Birla temple in Evening. Evening Optional Visit (Entry Fee Rs.1000 Per Head) to Chokhi Dhani - meaning ‘special village,’ is a mock Rajasthani village 20km south of Jaipur, and is a fun place to take the kids. There are open-air restaurants, where you can enjoy a tasty Rajasthani thali, and there’s a bevy of traditional entertainment – dancers, acrobats, snack stalls – as well as adventure park–like activities for kids to swing on, slide down and hide in, later after full enjoyment back to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 05 : Jaipur

Today morning visit Amber fort, enjoy Elephant Ride ( Optional ride on direct payment basis @ Rs.1000 per Elephant for 2 pax) Visit Durga Temple, Mirror Palace, Royal ladies apartments and many more in Amber fort, later visit Jalmahal & after lunch free for shopping. Evening drop at Jaipur Airport / Railway Station.
