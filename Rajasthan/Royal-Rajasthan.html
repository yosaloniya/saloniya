Royal Rajasthan Tour

3 Nights - 4 days

Day 01 : Udaipur

Welcome on arrival, transfer to hotel after fresh up visit City Palace - Standing on the bank of lake Pichola, are massive series of palaces built at different time commencing from 1559 A.D. The balconies of the palace provide panoramic view of "Jag Niwas" (Now world wide famous Lake palace hotel), Jag Mandir on one side and on the other the city of Udaipur, Jagdish Temple, Jag Mandir, Vintage Car Museum, Queen’s Garden, Lake Pichola enjoy boat ride , Evening transfer to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 02 : Udaipur - Jodhpur ( 7 Hrs )

Today morning after breakfast drive to Jodhpur, reach & transfer to hotel, after fresh-up visit Mehrangarh Fort this is the most glorious fort in the city of Jodhpur. Built in the year 1459 by Rao Jodha, the fort includes several palaces, temples, galleries and a museum within its premises. & Jashwant Thada - this is a royal white marbled cenotaph. Mainly built in the memory of Maharaja Jaswant Singh, this place even displays some of the rare portraits of Jodhpur's former rulers. Later visit Umed Bhawan Palace - Constructed in the 20th Century by Maharaja Umaid Singh, this is one of the captivating Jodhpur palaces. However, presently, a part of the Umaid Bhavan Palace has been transformed to a museum and a hotel. Evening free to explore Jodhpur markets & transfer to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 03 : Jodhpur - Jaisalmer ( 6 hrs )

Today morning drive to Jaisalmer, Visit Sonar Quila (Golden Fort) Jaisalmer Fort was erected by Raja Jaisal in the year of 1156. Amidst the picturesque landscape of the high hills of Trikuta, Jaisalmer Fort stands tall with its over imposing architectural structure with a height of 250 feet, later we will visit Jaisalmer Havelies – Nathmal Haveli, Patwon ki Haveli, Salim Singh Haveli & Gadisar Lake, evening drive to SAM Sand Dune & transfer to Desert camp resort. Enjoy Desert Camel Safari, Dinner with folk dance & Music in your resort. Overnight halt in Desert camp resort.

Day 04 : Jaisalmer - bikaner ( 6 Hrs )

Today morning after an early breakfast & check-out, drive to Bikaner, reach & transfer to hotel, later enjoy Bikaner sightseeing, Visit Junagarh Fort - It was built by Maharaja Rai Singh. The entire fort is steeped in deep history due to which it appeals to the tourists to a great extent. The fort is completely built with red sandstone brought from the Thar Desert. However, the interior work is done with marble. There are many wonderful things to see within the fort. The rich heritage of the king along with many temples and Havelis of the kings are found within the fort. Some of the palaces found here include Ganga Mahal, Phool Mahal, Badal Mahal, etc. There is also a fort museum within the fort and displays collection of dresses, weapons, paintings and many other things of historical importance. Later visit Lalgarh Palace, Camel Breeding Farm & Jain Temple. Later back to hotel for overnight halt.

Day 05 : Bikaner - Ajmer - Pushkar ( 5 Hrs )

Today morning drive to Pushkar, visit Karni Mata Temple also famous as Rat Temple of Bikaner, Reach & transfer to hotel in Pushkar, later visit Khwaja Sahib Dargah in Ajmer, Pushkar Lake & Bhrama Temple Overnight halt in Pushkar.

Day 06 : Pushkar - jaipur ( 3 Hrs )

Today morning drive to Jaipur, reach & transfer to Hotel, later visit Amber fort, enjoy Elephant Ride ( Optional ride on direct payment basis @ Rs.1000 per Elephant for 2 pax) Visit Durga Temple, Mirror Palace, Royal ladies apartments and many more in Amber fort, later visit Jaigarh Fort & Tiger fort. Evening free to explore Jaipur Markets.

Day 07 : Jaipur

Today morning visit City Palace (residence of present Royal Family of Jaipur), Jantar Mantar- is the largest and best preserved of Sawai Jai Singh's five observatories built in 1718, is a world heritage sight since 2008, later we will visit Hawa Mahal & Jalmahal, evening drive to Chokhi Dhani, visit Birla Temple on the way. Enjoy dinner with folk dance & music with other cultural programs. Later drive back to Hotel for overnight halt.

Day 08 : jaipur Drop

Today we will drop you at Jaipur Airport / Railway Station.
