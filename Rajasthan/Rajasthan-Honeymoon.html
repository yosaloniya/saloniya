Rajasthan Honeymoon Tour Package

9 Nights / 10 days

Day 01 : Arive in Delhi

Upon your arrival at the Delhi Airport, meet our tour representative. He will escort you to the hotel. After check-in, you can take some time to relax. Then, later in the day, you can go to explore the attractions of Delhi if you want to. Or else, you can spend the day relaxing and then have your delicious dinner. An overnight stay is planned at the hotel.

Day 02 : Delhi Sightseeing

Day 2 of your romantic Rajasthan vacation starts with the Delhi city tour. After breakfast, you will get ready to visit some of the top tourist attractions in Delhi. You will cover prominent attractions, including Jama Masjid and Raj Ghat. Later, have your lunch, and then you will move towards India Gate, the iconic place in Delhi. Next, visit the very popular Humayun's Tomb and the complex of Qutub Minar. Finally, call it a day at Red Fort while attending the beguiling light and sound show. Have your dinner and enjoy an overnight stay in a pre-booked hotel in Delhi. Enjoy Jaipur sightseeing tour Delight in an excursion at Samode.

Day 03 : Delhi - Agra ( 230km/4 hr 20 min-approx. )

On day 3, you will head to the next destination, Agra. Post breakfast, complete the checkout formalities, and your journey to the 'City of Taj' will begin. Upon reaching, you will be transferred to one of our listed hotels. Again, complete the check-in formalities and spend some to relax. Now that you have gained back some energy, it's time to go for a sightseeing tour of Agra. The first attraction is the Taj Mahal. The architectural marvel is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Next in line are Agra Fort, Akbar's tomb, and the Tomb of Itimad-Ud-Daulah. After the tour, head back to the hotel for dinner and the night's stay.

Day 04 : Agra - Samode

Once done with satiating your hunger with a delicious breakfast on day 4 of the Rajasthan Honeymoon Tour, checkout from the hotel. Post this, your road journey to Samode will begin. The route will take you via Fatehpur Sikri, where a quick stop at the ancient monument is planned. The journey resumes soon after you are done with the heritage tour. After reaching Samode, get comfortably transferred to your hotel. The rest of the day is to be enjoyed leisurely. Munch your dinner and enjoy an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 05 : Samode - Jaipur ( 52 km/1 hr 10 min-approx. )

After having your breakfast, get ready for a day trip at Samode. Spend the half-day marveling at the architectural brilliance of the palatial structures. Gorge on delicious lunch and hit the road to Jaipur. Also known as the 'Pink City, the town boasts innumerable forts and palaces. Upon arrival, complete the check-in formalities at the hotel and take some rest. After having your dinner, enjoy an overnight stay.

Day 06 : Jaipur City Tour

Begin your day with a tasty breakfast and embark on a sightseeing tour in Pink City. You will start this day by visiting the Amer Fort, one of the most iconic places in Jaipur. Once done exploring the fort, relish delicious lunch. Afterward, proceed towards the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar. Once you explored all the major tourist places, you will be taken to the local bazaars of Jaipur. From here you can buy lots of gifts and souvenirs for each other. Return to the hotel for a delicious dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 07 : Jaipur - Udaipur ( 393 km/6 hr 40 min-approx. )

After breakfast, you will depart by surface to Udaipur, which will take around 7 hrs. Approx. Upon arrival in Udaipur, you will have an assisted check-in at the hotel. The rest of the day is at your leisure. Enjoy the hotel facilities or delight in the majestic beauty of this remarkable place with your loved one. Munch your mouthwatering dinner and enjoy an overnight stay at the hotel in Udaipur.

Day 08 : Udaipur Day Trip

A day trip to Udaipur is reserved for Day 8 of your Rajasthan romantic tour package. But, first, make your way to the City Palace. A notable gem in the crown of Rajasthan. The afternoon is to be spent taking a boat ride on Lake Pichola. The romantic ride will be a memorable affair. The last attraction of the day will be Saheliyon ki, Bari, a major garden in Udaipur that flaunts a lotus pool. You can see beautiful fountains, kiosks, and marble elephants. Return to the hotel for dinner and enjoy an overnight stay in Udaipur.

Day 09 : Udaipur - Delhi ( 665 km/12 hr-approx. )

After breakfast, you will be transferred to the airport to connect the flight to Delhi. Arrived in Delhi and met with our tour representative. Have an assisted transfer to the hotel. The rest of the day at leisure - on your activity. Have a tasty dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel in Delhi.

Day 10 : Delhi - Departure

This day marks the end of your romantic holiday in Rajasthan journey. First, wake up early and munch your breakfast while enjoying the sunshine. Next, you can spend the day leisurely or do things entertaining you. Afterward, get ready to be escorted to the airport for your onward journey.
